About

Ted grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, attending Maplewood Bible Chapel and Fenton Crossing Bible Chapel. He now lives in Hillsboro, Missouri with his wife Lydia and their three children. For the past ten years, he has been part of Bethel Community Church, where he has served as a deacon for the last six years.

Ted teaches regularly at Bethel as well as Bible camps including Turkey Hill, Dayspring, and Storybook Lodge. By day, he works as a full-time software developer. Building websites for chapels is his evenings and weekends project—a way to serve assemblies with the skills God has given him.
